Mission Driven

Serving the Nation by Serving the Community

Legacy of Valor is a Texas Domestic Non-Profit Corporation and a federally recognized 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization based in El Paso, Texas. We operate as a premier information and referral hub, cutting through the red tape to provide veterans, their families, and the community with direct pathways to stability and success. Vets Helping Vets.

Our Core Pillars

Healthcare & Benefits

We guide veterans and their families through the complex landscape of healthcare access and VA benefits, ensuring no one is left behind by the system.

Career Support

Translating military excellence into civilian success. We provide actionable referrals and resources to help transitioning service members secure meaningful employment.

Entrepreneurship

Building the next generation of veteran leaders. We offer guidance and support for the start-up of small businesses and mission-driven non-profit ventures.
